Password Management

Too many passwords to remember?

A password manager means you'll only ever have to use one password. It will take care of the rest.

Contact me to find out more about the importance of having strong, unique passwords for each service that you use. And how I can help you get started.

Storage Space

Keep running out of space for your photos/videos?

Depending on your needs I could help set up cloud based storage or local storage.

A cloud storage solution will be cheaper in the short term and more convenient. Local storage is more expensive upfront but avoids the subscription.
